Follow these steps to run an experiment.

Note, MassSpring drive will unmount during this process. Copy these instructions to your computer before beginning if necessary.

1: Press the ZERO button
2: Move the mass to the desired initial displacement
3: Press the REC button (status light shows recording)
4: Release the mass and allow to oscillate.
5: Wait until REC light turns off.
6: Drive "MassSpring" appears on host computer, contains data file.
